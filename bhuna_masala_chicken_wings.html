BHUNA MASALA CHICKEN WINGS

log out

Ingredients for Bhuna Masala Chicken Wings

  • 1/2 kg chicken wings
  • 2 onions finely julienned
  • 3 tomatoes sliced
  • 1 capsicum finely julienned
  • 1 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 3 dry red chili
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 lime juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp refined oil
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil

Procedure to cook Bhuna Masala Chicken Wings

  • step 1

    Marinate the chicken wings.

    step 2

    Drizzle the chicken wings with olive oil. Then add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder, salt to taste and massage the chicken wings gently.

    step 3

    Heat up the pan and drizzle with one tsp oil and sear the wings in it, add salt to taste.

    step 4

    For bhuna masala:

    step 5

    Heat up the pan and drizzle it with 1 tsp of oil and add dry red chili, cumin seeds and coriander seeds.

    step 6

    Saute it lightly. Then mix chicken wings with the bhuna masala.

    step 7

    Add onions and saute it till golden brown.

    step 8

    Add tomatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes. Then add sugar, water, lime and capsicum and let it cook away for 2-3 minutes more.

    step 9

    Sprinkle chopped coriander leaves to garnish.
