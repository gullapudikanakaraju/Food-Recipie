BOOZY BLUEBERRY-MAPLE SHAKE

log out

Ingredients for Boozy Bluberry-Maple Shake

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 1/2 ounces gin, such as Beefeater
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup

Procedure to make Boozy Bluberry-Maple Shake

  • step 1

    Add the blueberries and gin to the blender. Pulse a few times then puree on medium high speed until smooth and no large pieces of blueberry skin are be visible, about 30 seconds.

    step 2

    Add ice cream and maple syrup and blend on medium high speed just until smooth, about 30 seconds. Serve immediately.
