SPIKED TAGALONG MILKSHAKE

log out

Ingredients for Spiked Tagalong Milkshake

  • 1 cup chocolate ice cream (about 6 ounces / 2 medium scoops)
  • 2 cups butter pecan ice cream (about 12 ounces / 4 medium scoops)
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 2 ounces bourbon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup smooth peanut butter
  • Chocolate syrup to line glass
  • Garnish: Whipped cream and chopped toasted peanuts

Procedure to make Spiked Tagalong Milkshake/p>

  • step 1

    Add chocolate ice cream, butter pecan ice cream, milk, bourbon, salt, and peanut butter to a blender. Purée until smooth.

    step 2

    Drizzle chocolate syrup on sides of serving glasses. Divide blended milkshake among glasses, top with whipped cream and toasted peanuts.
